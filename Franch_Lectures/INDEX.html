What You'll Learn

By Day 04:

Recognize and discuss 15 automotive parts (brakes, powertrain, body). Understand the French article system (definite vs indefinite). Participate in professional discussions about brake system maintenance.

By Day 05:

Master the French number system, including the unique rules for 70, 80, and 90. Read and communicate technical measurements in French. Discuss suspension specifications with French-speaking colleagues.

By Day 06:

Write professional emails in French with proper formality levels. Use 20 essential email expressions in context. Submit technical reports and correspond with French colleagues professionally.

By Day 07:

Consolidate Days 01-06 learning with comprehensive review. Understand the neuroscience behind effective language learning. Self-assess your mastery across all lessons. Feel confident entering Day 08 (meeting facilitation) topics.
